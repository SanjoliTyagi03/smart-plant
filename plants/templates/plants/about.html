The journey

How PlantCare
Came to Life

The Beginning 🛒

I fell in love with plants

Walking through a nursery one afternoon, I bought my first three plants on impulse — a Pothos, a Peace Lily, and a little succulent. They looked effortless. I thought plants were just… decorations that stayed alive on their own.

The Wake-Up Call 😟

They started dying

Within two months, the Peace Lily had yellow leaves. The succulent turned to mush. The Pothos started wilting. I was devastated. I was watering them daily — out of love — not knowing I was literally drowning them. Nobody had warned me.

The Search 🔍

The internet only confused me more

I searched endlessly — "why is my Pothos dying", "how often should I water a succulent". Every answer was buried under ads, contradictions, and forum arguments. There was no single, clear, trustworthy place I could just go and learn.

The Idea 💡

What if I built the thing I needed?

After losing my fifth plant, something shifted. Instead of feeling defeated, I felt determined. I wanted one place — clean, honest, and human — with real care guides, easy search, and even AI to help diagnose what's wrong. PlantCare was born.

The Build ⌨️

I coded it, one plant at a time

I learned as I went — researching every plant I added, cross-checking with botanists and growers, writing care instructions I wish I'd had from day one. The Analyze feature came from one frustrating afternoon of not knowing what was wrong with a leaf.

Today 🌿

All my plants are thriving

And so is PlantCare. What started as a personal project is now a growing community of plant parents who deserve better than guesswork. If you're reading this, you're already one of us. Welcome.

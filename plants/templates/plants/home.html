care of your plants

The right solution for the care of green and smart plants

Watering Schedule

Water deeply but infrequently. Let soil dry between waterings to avoid root rot — when in doubt, wait a day.

Pest Prevention

Inspect leaves regularly for pests. Early detection makes treatment straightforward and keeps plants thriving.

Pruning & Trimming

Remove dead or yellowing leaves promptly. Regular pruning encourages new growth and maintains your plant's shape.
